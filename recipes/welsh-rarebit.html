Welsh rarebit

Description

Welsh Rarebit is a British dish consisting of an ale-laced cheese based sauce served over slices of delicious grilled bread

Ingredients

  • 25g butter
  • 2 tsp flour
  • 100ml dark beer
  • 1 tsp English mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 150g strong, mature English cheddar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 slices country bread
  • 1 tsp chopped chives

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.Stir in the flour, and cook for 1 minute without browning.
  2. Add the beer one tbsp at a time and whisk until smooth after each addition. Continue to whisk until thick, then whisk in mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and whisk in the cheese until thick and smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning
  4. Remove from the heat for a minute to cool slighty before whisking in egg yolk
  5. Spread thickly on toast, dust with freshly ground black pepper and place under a hot broiler until bubbling golden brown.
  6. Garnish with chives and serve while hot
